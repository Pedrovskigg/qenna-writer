The editor

Writing software that gets out of your way

Focus Mode, inline comments, cross-document tagging with @, and a style reviewer that flags repetition and weak verbs — without an AI rewriting your voice for you.

Every story, organized your way

Drawers for characters, settings, objects, and lore. Multiple manuscripts in a single project — write a whole trilogy without losing track of book one.

  • Unlimited documents, folders, and cross-references
  • Reference Menu — jump to any chapter or scene instantly
  • Search across the current document, project, or reference panel

Never lose the thread

Automatic character presence detection across chapters and scenes, participation graphs, and a full dialogue log per character — know exactly who said what, and where.

  • Character relationship & bonds graph
  • Consistency tracking for objects and settings too
  • The Pensarium — save any passage as a searchable memory

Plan before you write a single line

The Board is a corkboard for plotting — acts, arcs, storylines. The Builder helps you design magic systems, religions, and political structures. The Timeline logs every event automatically.

  • Export any card, note, or planning area straight into a drawer
  • Tangled or radial timeline views
  • Glossary for invented words and worldbuilding terms
